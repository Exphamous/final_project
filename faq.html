Frequently Asked Questions

This is where you will find most answers. If you have any further questions, don't hesitate to contact us.

Do we provide member parking?

We have free street parking and 2 private parking lots available.

One is on the side of the gym and the other is located behind the gym.

How old do i have to be to join the gym?

There is a minimum age requirement of 16 years old at Fogo de Gym.

16 & 17-year-olds are to be accompanied by a guardian.

Do you offer personal training?

Yes, we have a personal training program.

Our trainers are cheering you on and provide you with the support you need to achieve your dream body, with specializations in body building, weight loss, nutrition, injury rehabilitation and more! Visit our personal training page to learn more.

Are group fitness classes included with my membership?

All group fitness classes are included with your Fogo de Gym membership.
